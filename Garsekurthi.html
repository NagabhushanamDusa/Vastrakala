Garshakurthy Silk Saree Collection

Garshakurthy is a handloom weaving village located in Karimnagar district, Telangana. Around 2,000 families in this village are involved in handloom weaving, producing cotton sarees every day. The weavers of Garshakurthy are well-known for fulfilling government handloom orders, supporting Telangana’s traditional textile industry. Garshakurthy sarees are made using pure cotton yarn on traditional handlooms. The village plays an important role in preserving handloom heritage, providing employment to rural artisans, and promoting sustainable and eco-friendly textile production.

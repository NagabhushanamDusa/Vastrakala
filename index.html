Meet the Weavers

Banarasi Silk – Uttar Pradesh

Banarasi silk weavers from Varanasi are known for their intricate zari work and rich traditional designs. Their craftsmanship reflects centuries of weaving heritage passed down through generations.

Kanchipuram Silk – Tamil Nadu

Kanchipuram weavers create heavy, durable silk sarees with bright colors and temple-inspired patterns. These sarees are a symbol of South Indian tradition and wedding fashion.

Pochampally Silk – Telangana

Pochampally weavers are famous for their unique geometric designs and Ikat dyeing technique. Each saree is carefully hand-dyed and woven with precision.

Garshakurthy Silk – Telangana

The weavers of Garshakurthy are well-known for fulfilling government handloom orders, supporting Telangana’s traditional textile industry. Garshakurthy sarees are made using pure cotton yarn on traditional handlooms.
